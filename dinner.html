Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Spaghetti Squash Casserole

  • Roast spaghetti squash until tender, then shred into strands.
  • Cook chicken in a skillet, then mix with sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and a creamy sauce.
  • Combine the chicken mixture with spaghetti squash and bake until golden.

Kathi Roll

  • Prepare a spiced filling with paneer, chicken, or vegetables.
  • Roll the filling in a soft paratha with sauces and onions.
  • Grill lightly and serve hot.
