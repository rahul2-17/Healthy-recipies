Lunch

Veg Biryani

  • Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • Prepare biryani masala using cinnamon, cloves, cardamoms, and mace.
  • Cook vegetables with the masala and mix with rice for a flavorful biryani.

Kathi Roll

  • Prepare a spiced filling with paneer, chicken, or vegetables.
  • Roll the filling in a soft paratha with sauces and onions.
  • Grill lightly and serve hot.

Ghee Rice

  • Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • Prepare a tempering of ghee, spices, and onions.
  • Cook rice with the tempering for a rich and aromatic dish.

Dinner

Pasta with Garlicky Tomato Sauce & Bay Scallops

  • Cook spaghetti and set aside with reserved pasta water.
  • Cook scallops until lightly browned and set aside.
  • Prepare tomato sauce with garlic, tomatoes, and basil.
  • Mix pasta with sauce and scallops, cook until well combined.

Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken Spaghetti Squash Casserole

  • Roast spaghetti squash and shred into strands.
  • Cook chicken with sun-dried tomatoes and creamy sauce.
  • Combine with spaghetti squash and bake until golden.
